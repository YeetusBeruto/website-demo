Creation of SultronGames



SultronGames is a tech company created in 2020 by Christoforos Anyfantis for a college project

however it quickly found success and Anyfantis decided to develop his idea into the now well known "SultronGames"

During it's early stages, Anyfantis worked alone from his bedroom with a simple laptop and a couple tools,

however the company's success through his online website allowed him to afford a team of tech wizards and an apartment to work from.

From 2020 up until now, SultronGames has reached over a million sales worldwide and continues making the dream of gamers into a reality.
